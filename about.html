About

Founded by Yulie and Blevyn Nutzenbågen. Trusted by travelers in over 60 countries.

The Company

The Nut BAGs™ is a global leader in portable snack containment systems.

What began as a practical solution to a common travel problem has evolved into a category-defining company with a singular focus: bringing order, dignity, and intention to nuts on the move.

The company operates at scale with vertically integrated manufacturing and a research-first culture.

The Founders

Yulie Nutzenbågen
Co-Founder & Chief Research Officer

Yulie Nutzenbågen built her career at the intersection of nutritional science and behavioral research. Her work examined portion control, transport integrity, and the cognitive effects of reliable access to nuts in high-stress environments.

Earlier in life, Yulie was a founding member of Shell Shock Doctrine. The band's single briefly charted on an independent European Top 100 list.

She continues to oversee all research initiatives at The Nut BAGs™.

"When you control the container, you influence the outcome. The data supports this."
— Yulie Nutzenbågen
Blevyn Nutzenbågen
Co-Founder & Chief Product Officer

Blevyn Nutzenbågen is a serial entrepreneur and product inventor. Prior to The Nut BAGs™, he gained recognition for the Spherical Foam Acoustic Modulation Device™, designed to manage aggressive consonants and unpredictable breath events in live audio environments.

He is also credited with the invention of Noise-Canceling Ice Cubes, developed to reduce disruptive clinking in premium cocktails.

Blevyn leads product strategy with a focus on materials, ergonomics, and restraint.

"People think these are simple objects. That's because they don't understand the problem."
— Blevyn Nutzenbågen
